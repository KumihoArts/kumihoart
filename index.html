About

Music That Breathes Life Into Your Game

I'm Kumiho — a game music composer specialising in atmospheric, emotionally-driven scores for indie games, visual novels, and interactive experiences. My sound draws from cinematic orchestration, dark ambience, and folklore-infused tonality.

Whether you need a haunting dungeon theme, an epic boss encounter, or an intimate character moment, I craft music that pulls players deeper into your world.
